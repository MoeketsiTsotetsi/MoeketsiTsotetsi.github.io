Project Overview

The Football Management Platform simplifies football operations by giving coaches, managers, and players real-time access to team and player data.

  • Player registration and profile management
  • Team creation and roster management
  • Match scheduling and result tracking
  • Performance analytics and statistics
  • Secure Express.js REST API with JWT auth
  • Responsive React Native app for Android & iOS
