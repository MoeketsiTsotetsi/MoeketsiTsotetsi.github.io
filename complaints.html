Complaints Management System

A system to log, track, and manage customer complaints efficiently with analytics and reporting.

Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

The Complaints Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline the process of handling customer complaints. It provides a centralized platform for logging, tracking, and resolving complaints while offering valuable insights through analytics.

Objective

To create an efficient, user-friendly system that reduces complaint resolution time and improves customer satisfaction through transparent tracking and timely updates.

Target Users

Customer service representatives, managers, and customers needing to submit or track complaints through a self-service portal.

Outcomes

Reduced resolution time by 40%, increased customer satisfaction scores, and provided valuable data for improving products and services.

Key Features

  • Log new complaints with detailed information and categorization
  • Track status of complaints (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed)
  • Assign complaints to specific team members or departments
  • Set and track resolution deadlines with automated reminders
  • Internal communication system for collaboration on complaints
  • Customer portal to submit and track complaints
  • Automated notifications and status updates via email/SMS
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics dashboard
  • Knowledge base integration for common solutions
  • Customer satisfaction surveys after resolution
